Mission

We offer a specific methodology that adapts a contemporary Montessori methodology. This kind of system strives for a balance between academic achievement and a strong value system protecting the concerns of the child. Through the Plumeria Montessori Method, children are encouraged to learn through practical experience and self-discovery within a carefully prepared child-oriented environment. Classrooms are equipped with scientifically prepared material for each stage of development and the trained teachers guide the children to develop a well-adjusted, balanced personality.
