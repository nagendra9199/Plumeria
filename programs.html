OUR PROGRAMS

We offer a specific methodology that adapts a contemporary Montessori values. This kind of system strives for a balance between academic achievement and a strong value system protecting the concerns of the child. Through the Plumeria Montessori Method, children are encouraged to learn through practical experience and self-discovery within a carefully prepared child-oriented environment. Classrooms are equipped with scientifically prepared material for each stage of development and the trained teachers guide the children to develop a well-adjusted, balanced personality. Every aspect of learning and needs of the child are deeply questioned and enriched by the teacher, the participation of the parent and the inclusion of the community.

Play Group

Pre-Nursery program focuses on play-way based learning methodology. Play is an essential part of the preschool experience and it provides opportunities for children to explore, experiment, and learn in a natural and enjoyable way. It fosters curiosity and creativity.

Nursery (3 Years+ Onwards)

Plumeria Nursery Program lays the foundation for all the key skills desirable for an early learner. Children are taught re-writing, pre-reading, pre-math, science and social skills in a natural logical sequence, encouraging the little ones to learn one step at a time. A well-planned curriculum spanning monthly thematic programs, specific weekly topics and daily learning schedules keep the child focused on academic learning through explorations. “A child who has become master of his acts through long and repeated exercises, and who has been encouraged by the pleasant and interesting activities in which he has been engaged, is a child filled with health and joy and remarkable for his calmness and discipline.”

Key Areas of Development

Gross & Fine motor skills.

Communication skills.

Pre writing skills (Alphabet & Numbers).

Cognitive development.

Math’s & Numbers.

Phonemic awareness.

Life skills.

Grace & courtesy.

Creative expression through art, craft, role play & dance.

Kindergarten (4 & 5 Years + Onwards)

Plumeria kindergarten Programs are unique in the way we prepare the child holistically and well ahead for primary school education. The program is thoughtfully curated to facilitate a smooth transition from preschool to a structured learning environment, setting the stage for formal school. All the skills, he learning and information gathering the child has acquired in the preceding two years of the Playgroup & Nursery programs are assimilated, reinforced and come to fruition in these transformative years. “There are many who hold, as I do, that the most important period of life is not the age of university studies, but the first one, the period from birth to the age of six. For that is the time when man's intelligence itself, his greatest implement, is being formed. But not only his intelligence; the full totality of his psychic powers."

Child Care

The half-day and full-day child care programs offer a healthy mix of physical activities, meal time, nap time, and educational opportunities. We have a dedicated nap room for our toddlers and a fully functional pantry to support their meal time. The program allows children to engage in age-appropriate activities and provides time to complete school home work. Children will have opportunities to develop skills in various subjects and be encouraged to practice their hobbies. Our childcare is a great way to get regular updates, including observations and pictures. It also gives parents a comprehensive daily routine of their child's activity in daycare. We offer after-school programs that are important for children to learn and provide families with the assurance and support they need as they head to work. This program introduces children to concepts like practical life- skills development, nature club, financial literacy, drama, storytelling and many others.

Healthy environment & Nutritious food

Clean beds & wash rooms give comfort to your child. Healthy, and nutritious home cooked food gives motherly feel. Care givers provides personal attention to every child.
