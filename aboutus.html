Vision

Plumeria is a contemporary school with a balance of academic learning, concern for the child and a strong value system. Our curriculum provides a holistic, child-centered approach to nourish the children’s brain, Heart, and Hands, as we develop Cognitive, Social, Emotional, and Physical skills through varied experiences.

Mission

We offer a specific methodology that adapts a contemporary Montessori values. This kind of system strives for a balance between academic achievement and a strong value system protecting the concerns of the child. Through our method, children are encouraged to learn through by practical experience and self-discovery within a carefully prepared child-oriented environment. Classrooms are equipped with scientifically prepared material for each stage of development and the trained teachers guide the children to develop a well-adjusted, balanced personality.

Every aspect of learning and needs of the child are deeply questioned and enriched by the teacher, the participation of the parent and the inclusion of the community. The school’s approach to pre-primary education is grounded on activity-based learning which helps foster and stimulate healthy inquisitiveness in the tiny tots. The school tries to build social skills at an early age and the children learn to be open minded and unprejudiced towards their peers and to collaborate with them.
