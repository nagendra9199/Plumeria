OUR MISSION

We unite to bring in the skills, experience and ethical practices in special education to the families having children with autism and other developmental disabilities to find a constructive and independent life.

OUR VISION

We aim to provide education with practical learning experiences and vocational opportunities to children and young adults with diverse needs to explore their inner abilities to be future ready.
