PREPARE YOUR CHILD FOR THE BEST FUTURE!!!

Welcome to Plumeria Kindergarten, a place where education is viewed as a journey of self-discovery, curiosity, and independent exploration. At our Montessori school, we embrace the philosophy and methodology which recognizes the unique potential with in each child. At Plumeria, we understand that the early years are a crucial period of growth and development, and we are dedicated to providing a safe, stimulating, and supportive environment for your child. Our passionate & experienced educators are committed to fostering a love for learning and helping each child reach their full potential. Our mission is to create an environment that fosters a love of learning, independence, and a deep sense of respect for oneself, others, and the world around us.

About Us

“Our classrooms are peaceful, happy places designed to meet the developmental needs of each child in every stage of life.”

Plumeria (which means a symbol of new life). We offer a dynamic learning environment designed to meet the needs of various age groups and to make learning an exciting and continuous process. Programs

Early Development is fundamental to the growth of your child. starts with our daycare!!

Play Group

Pre-Nursery program focuses on play-way based learning methodology. Play is an essential part of the preschool experience and it provides opportunities for children to explore, experiment, and learn in a natural and enjoyable way. It fosters curiosity and creativity.

Nursery

Nursery program focuses on early learning skills and the curriculum include activities related to pre-reading and pre-math skills, language development, and cognitive development. Also, prioritize the development of social and emotional skills which are key components of child growth, that help children understand and manage their emotions. Literacy concepts are introduced which includes letter recognition, phonemic awareness, and storytelling, all of which prepare them for reading in kindergarten and beyond.

Kindergarten

Kindergarten 1 & 2 classes: Children are exposed to a rich language environment, expanding their vocabulary, improving communication skills, and building the foundation for later literacy development. Children will be introduced to basic math concepts through counting, sorting, and recognizing shapes and patterns. These activities set the stage for more formal math education in elementary school. Children are encouraged to think critically, make choices, and solve simple problems. These skills lay the groundwork for analytical thinking and decision-making in the future.

Child Care

In this age of women empowerment, it is common to see households with working couples/single parents constantly on the lookout for quality day care-givers for their school going children. Parents are often desiring for trusted and a better professional care for their precious one. No parent can work in peace if they are constantly worried about the quality of care being provided to their little one.

We are Dedicated to Provide

In our school, children are taught how to regulate their own social interactions. Through fun role-playing activities and appropriate modelling, the teacher demonstrates the best way to respond to arguments or new situations, giving the child the ability to act confidently and pro-socially when the actual problem arises. The result is a self-regulating classroom, in which natural social tensions are resolved mostly by the children themselves.
