Pre-Nursery

The Pre-Nursery program focuses on play-way based learning methodology. Play is an essential part of the preschool experience and it provides opportunities for children to explore, experiment, and learn in a natural and enjoyable way. It fosters curiosity and creativity.

Nursery

Nursery program focuses on early learning skills and the curriculum include activities related to pre-reading and pre-math skills, language development, and cognitive development. Also, prioritize the development of social and emotional skills which are key components of child growth, that help children understand and manage their emotions

Kindergarten 1 (K1)

Children are exposed to a rich language environment, expanding their vocabulary, improving communication skills, and building the foundation for later literacy development. Children will be introduced to basic math concepts through counting, sorting, and recognizing shapes and patterns.

Kindergarten 2 (K2)

These activities set the stage for more formal math education in elementary school. Children are encouraged to think critically, make choices, and solve simple problems. These skills lay the groundwork for analytical thinking and decision-making in the future.
