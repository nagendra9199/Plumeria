PREPARE YOUR CHILD FOR THE BEST FUTURE!!!

Welcome to Plumeria Kindergarten, a place where education is viewed as a journey of self-discovery, curiosity, and independent exploration. At our Montessori school, we embrace the philosophy and methodology which recognizes the unique potential with in each child. At Plumeria, we understand that the early years are a crucial period of growth and development, and we are dedicated to providing a safe, stimulating, and supportive environment for your child. Our passionate & experienced educators are committed to fostering a love for learning and helping each child reach their full potential. Our mission is to create an environment that fosters a love of learning, independence, and a deep sense of respect for oneself, others, and the world around us.

About Us

“Our classrooms are peaceful, happy places designed to meet the developmental needs of each child in every stage of life.”

Plumeria (which means a symbol of new life). We offer a dynamic learning environment designed to meet the needs of various age groups and to make learning an exciting and continuous process. At Plumeria, we respect each and every child’s uniqueness, recognize their abilities and most importantly we give them freedom to accomplish what they want.

Programs

Play Group

Pre-Nursery program focuses on play-way based learning methodology. Play is an essential part of the preschool experience and it provides opportunities for children to explore, experiment, and learn in a natural and enjoyable way. It fosters curiosity and creativity and improves child’s adaptation to the environment.

Nursery

Nursery program focuses on early learning skills and the curriculum include activities related to pre-reading and pre-math skills, language development, and cognitive development. Social and emotional skills and literacy concepts are introduced which includes letter recognition, phonemic awareness, and storytelling.

Kindergarten

Children are exposed to a rich language environment, expanding their vocabulary, improving communication skills, and building the foundation for later literacy development. Children will be introduced to basic math concepts through counting, sorting, and recognizing shapes and patterns. Children are encouraged to think critically, make choices, and solve simple problems.

Child care

Mother’s need not end your career as childcare providers bring change in your life and help continue your career. We offer a trusted and a better professional care for their loved ones. Our child care helps for the all-round development of the child with nutritious food and clean environment.

We are Dedicated to Provide

In our school, children are taught how to regulate their own social interactions. Through fun role-playing activities and appropriate modelling, the teacher demonstrates the best way to respond to arguments or new situations, giving the child the ability to act confidently and pro-socially when the actual problem arises. The result is a self-regulating classroom, in which natural social tensions are resolved mostly by the children themselves.
